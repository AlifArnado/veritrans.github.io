Getting Started

client_key & server_key

To communicate with VT-Direct, Veritrans will provide merchants with two keys: client_key and server_key.

The client_key is a public key and is utilized for every Token request. The server_key is a private key that needs to be kept secret by the merchant. The server_key is utilized by the merchant to send a transaction request to Veritrans.

You can find your client_key and server_key here. Please use the Development Configuration while you are still in development. You will need to replace the client_key and server_key with the ones for Production once you are ready to go live.
