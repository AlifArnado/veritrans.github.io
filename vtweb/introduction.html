Introduction VT-Web

Target: Merchant understands VT-Web dan and its functions

VT-Web facilitates a payment feature for your online store. By using VT-Web, your customer is redirected from the online store to the Veritrans payment page for processing.

Veritrans payment page will assist your customer to complete his transaction with various payment methods such as credit cards, Mandiri ClickPay, and CIMB Clicks in a secure environment. At the same, we will help you reduce the labour of your technical team in the integration of the Veritrans system.
