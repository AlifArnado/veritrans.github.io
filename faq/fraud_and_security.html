How does Veritrans protect merchants against fraud?

Veritrans partners with Retail Decisions (ReD), a leading fraud detection provider. ReD detects fraud for $10 billion worth of transactions globally. ReD clients include Singapore Airlines, Walmart.com, and Sony Playstation, among others.

How does your fraud detection system work?

Each merchant is assigned to a specific set of fraud detection rules. These rules include checks on IP address location, transaction frequency, and size. Every transaction needs to pass this set of rules and depending on the outcome, a transaction will be approved, denied or challenged. For all challenged transactions, the merchant can decide to accept or deny the transaction depending on the merchant's risk tolerance. The merchant can conduct a manual review of the transaction by calling the customer if they wish. In order to verify the transaction, we will review with the merchant directly, check social networks for the identity of the buyer and check the credit card information. Only after all other options have been exhausted, we will contact the buyer. We understand that the priority is the user experience so we will do all we can to minimize contact with the buyer directly.

So are we safe from fraud?

While utilizing our fraud detection system will significantly reduce online fraud (less than 1%), we cannot promise that it will eliminate it entirely. Unfortunately, online fraud is a business risk that online merchants need to take (similar to bad account receivables). If merchant's are significantly adverse to fraud, we recommend merchants to implement 3D-secure, which provides merchant protection from fraud loss.

What is 3D Secure?

3D Secure is an online service designed to make online shopping transactions safer by authenticating a cardholder's identity at the time of purchase. This service is known as Verified by Visa and MasterCard SecureCode. A transaction using Verified by Visa/SecureCode will redirect cardholders to the website of their card issuing bank. The cardholder may then be requested by their bank to enter a password to be authenticated. If a customer is not registered with 3D Secure then they are still able to make a purchase from your website. However, given the additional steps that a consumer will need to go through to complete the transaction, 3D Secure may negatively impact your conversion rate and turn away legitimate customers. Please discuss with us if you are interested in 3D Secure or would like to learn more about it.

What is the average acceptance and denial rate?

The industry average for acceptance rate is 73-79%, ReD average acceptance rate is 91-96%, significantly higher than industry average. The industry average for denial rate is 2-4%, ReD average is 1-2%. The chargeback average from Retail Decisions is 0.2%, much lower than industry average, 0.9%.

Are you PCI-DSS Compliant?

Yes, we are certified as a PCI DSS Level 1 Service Provider, which means we process your cardholder data securely based on a globally accepted international standard.
