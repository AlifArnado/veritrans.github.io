How do I manage my account with Veritrans?

Veritrans has created a Merchant Administration Portal in which merchants can check transactions, check account balance, and request withdraw money. Merchants can also update their information on the MAP directly.

Withdrawing Money

A merchant can withdraw money from the account 7 days after a transaction is completed. There are no fees to withdraw money unless you want us to transfer to a bank account other than that of your acquiring bank.

Is there any minimum volume and ticket size ?

There is no minimum volume as we aim to make it easy for both small and large merchants to accept payments online. You may expect that by giving customers different payment options and enhancing the customer experience that your transaction volume will actually grow. In terms of ticket size, our fee structure of 3.9% + Rp 2,200 for credit cards may not make sense for merchants that have an average ticket size of less than Rp. 100,000. This is especially true if your margins are already very slim. We will work together with you to figure out the best payment solution.

What happen if there is different transactions data between merchant and veritrans ?

We will compare and adhere to the transaction log held by the bank.

Do you separate authorization and settlement? So that merchant have time to confirm the transactions?

Yes we can separate authorization and settlement period. The settlement will be held the next day (H+1) at 17:00pm. Merchant can void transaction any time before settlement if for example, the product is out of stock.

Can I have sub merchants?

Yes you can, you will have full responsibility for the sub merchants. Veritrans will only deal with you directly.

Can I use veritrans to accept international payment?

Yes, Veritrans can accept international credit cards with Visa or Mastercard logo.

Do you offer other types of payments?

Besides credit card, we also provide online banking (Mandiri ClickPay, CIMB Clicks, BCA KlikPay) and mobile payment (Telkomsel Cash, XL Tunai). We are in the midst of establishing partnership with other payment options. We will let you know once it has been finalized. Please stay tuned!

Do you offer monthly recurring payments?

Unfortunately, we currently do not offer recurring payments. However, we are working on it and hope to have a solution ready this year.

Do you offer mobile version?

Our payment page is already mobile enabled, so customers can do transaction using their smartphone and tablets.

Do you offer installment?

Yes, we can provide installment for BNI. We are working to provide other installment, and this should be ready sometime soon.

What will appear in our customer's credit card statement?

Your merchant name will appear in the statement.
