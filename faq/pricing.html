How much does Veritrans cost?

Our credit card fee for VT Web product is 3.9% + Rp. 2,000 (excl. PPN) of each transaction amount. There are no setup fees. For example, if you receive transaction Rp. 100.000 by credit card, the fee would be Rp 5,900 (Rp. 3,900 + Rp. 2,000). We do not charge for failed transactions. You only pay when you make money. We also have different pricing for online banking and mobile payments. Please see Pricing page for details. We believe that the eCommerce market will grow and we want to help it. We are trying to be the lowest cost payment gateway and transfer our reduced cost to add merchants profit.

Is the fee negotiable?

We want to be your partner to help your business grow and maximize revenues. We understand that different merchants have different needs in terms of margin levels, security requirements, transactions volume and more. Please feel free to contact us if you have any concern or question on the fee.

When do I receive the money in my account?

Veritrans transfers the money into the merchant bank account 7 days after the transaction is completed. All transfers to the merchant bank account can be viewed on the Merchant Administration Portal (MAP). Veritrans holds the money for 7 days to hold a reserve in case of any fraudulent activity.

Which bank do you use? Is there any fee if I send it to another bank account ?

We are currently connected to BNI, CIMB, and Mandiri, as the credit card acquiring banks. Merchants can choose which bank they would like to work with. If your account is the same as that of your acquiring bank, there will be no fees involved in transferring your money to your account. If you would like us to transfer the money to bank that is different from your acquiring bank, we will charge Rp. 3.500 per transfer, just enough to allow us to cover the bank charges. Please contact us for more details.

How does this pricing compare to other providers?

It is difficult to make a direct comparison. But most providers charge an additional set up fee, monthly fees, fraud detection services and other charges. Veritrans promises that there are no hidden charges.

Does the fee already include the Bank's Merchant Discount Rate?

Yes, it already includes the bank's Merchant Discount Rate (MDR).

Am I charged for refunds?

Veritrans processes refunds for free but the initial transaction charge cannot be returned. For example, if a customer pays Rp. 100,000 and requests a refund, the customer will receive Rp. 100,000 back. The merchant would have received Rp. 94,100 and will need to return the full Rp. 100,000 to the customer. The merchant incurs a net loss of the processing fee (3.9% + Rp. 2,000).

Am I charged for chargebacks?

No, we do not have chargeback fee for merchants. However, the merchant will need to refund the money to credit card holder for that particular transaction that is in dispute.
