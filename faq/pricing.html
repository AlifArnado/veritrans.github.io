How much does Veritrans cost?

Our credit card fee is 4% plus Rp. 2,000 (excl. PPN) of the transaction amount. There are no monthly or setup fees. For example, if you send Rp. 100.000 by credit card, the fee would be Rp 6,000 (Rp.4,000 + Rp. 2,000). We do not charge for declined transactions - you only pay when you make money. We believe that the eCommerce market will grow and we want to help it grows. We are trying to be the lowest cost payment gateway and transfer our reduced cost to add merchants profit.

Is the fee negotiable?

We want to be your partner to help your business grow and maximize revenues. We understand that different merchants have different needs in terms of margin levels, security requirements, transaction volume and more. Please feel free to contact us if you have any concern or question on the fee.

When do I receive the money in my account?

Veritrans transfers the money into the merchant bank account 7 days after the transaction is completed. A merchant can then withdraw the money from the account. There are no fees to withdraw money. All transfers to the merchant bank account can be viewed on the Merchant Administration Portal (MAP). Veritrans holds the money for 7 days to hold a reserve in case of any fraudulent activity.

Which bank do you use? Is there any fee if I send it to another bank account ?

We are currently connected to CIMB and BNI, and merchants can choose which bank they would like to work with. If your account is the same as that of your acquiring bank, there will be no fees involved in transferring your money to your account. If you would like us to remit the money to bank that is different from BNI or CIMB, we will charge Rp. 5000 per transfer, just enough to allow us to cover the bank charges. If you are an enterprise level merchant with transaction volumes above Rp 1 billion per month, your arrangement will be slightly different. Please contact us for more details.

How does this pricing compare to other providers?

It is difficult to make a direct comparison. But most providers charge an additional set up fee, monthly fees, fraud detection services and other charges. Veritrans promises that there are no hidden charges.

Does the fee already include the Bank's Merchant Discount Rate?

Yes, it already includes the bank's Merchant Discount Rate (MDR).

Am I charged for refunds?

Veritrans processes refunds for free but the initial transaction charge cannot be returned. For example, if a customer pays Rp. 100,000 and requests a refund, the customer will receive Rp. 100,000 back. The merchant would have received Rp. 95,000 and will need to return the full Rp. 100,000 to the customer. The merchant incurs a net loss of the processing fee (4% + Rp. 2,000).

Am I charged for chargebacks?

No, merchants are not charged for chargebacks. However, the merchant will need to refund the money for that particular transaction that is in dispute.
