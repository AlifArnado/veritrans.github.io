How quickly does it take to integrate with Veritrans?

It takes 10 working days, once all the necessary documentation has been received.

What information do I need to provide to integrate with Veritrans?

It will depend on the bank, but a typical merchant will need to provide the following documents:

  • Copy of SIUP
  • Copy of your latest bank account statement
  • Copy of Akta Pendirian
  • Copy of Nomor Pokok Wajib Pajak (NPWP)
  • Copy of letter from BKPM (if a foreign entity)
  • Copy of the KTP of company director
  • Merchant application form

Please contact us for more details about the document requirements.

How does your system work?

Our program will redirect your customers to our secure payment page. The benefit is you don't have to store any cardholder data in your website so you don't have to be PCI Compliant. Please check the technical documentation on the MAP for more details on how it works.

Can I customize the payment page?

We have optimized the payment page to make sure that customers will have a smooth payment experience, so while customization will be limited we still allow our merchants to add their own logo and colors to the payment page. This way, customers will know that they are buying from the merchant's website.

What kind of API/plug-ins do you provide?

We already have plugins for PHP, Ruby, Java, C#. For CMS frameworks, we have Drupal Commerce, VirtueMart, PrestaShop, WordPress (WooCommerce), OpenCart, and Magento. We may fulfill requests for any new plug-ins on a case-by-case basis.
