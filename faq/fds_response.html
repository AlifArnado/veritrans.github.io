What does a CHALLENGE transaction mean, does it charge my credit card?

Challenge transaction means that the transaction has been successfully authorized, but is being questioned by Veritrans fraud detection system. The credit card is not charged, but the funds are reserved. For every CHALLENGE transaction, merchant should ACCEPT or DENY the CHALLENGE transaction.

What should I do if there is a CHALLENGE transaction?

Merchant should ACCEPT or DENY the transaction.

Is a CHALLENGE transaction fraudulent?

Merchant should note that a CHALLENGE transaction means that the particular transaction has hit one or more of the rules that was set by Veritrans.

What happens if I do not ACCEPT or DENY my CHALLENGE transactions?

Even though the transaction is authorized, every CHALLENGE transaction that is not responded will be automatically CANCELLED during settlement time, meaning the credit card will NOT be charged.

Why cannot Veritrans just ACCEPT/DENY my CHALLENGE transactions on the merchant's behalf?

There are a number of reasons why Veritrans does not do this. Mainly because merchants know better of their business and customer, so they are in a better position to judge if the transaction should be accepted or denied. Secondly, it is easier to align internally with merchant's customer service if there are questions from customers afterwards.

When do I need to cancel a successful transaction? What is the typical scenario for me to do this?

There are many scenarios on when you need to do this. For example, lets say your customer purchased a brand new pair of shoes from your e-commerce website. Your inventory list is not updated so the shoes are apparently out of stock. You have informed the customer and he/she does not want to wait until the stock is ready and wants to cancel the transaction instead.

Can I cancel a transaction that has been settled (goes through settlement process)?

Unfortunately once a transaction is settled, it means that the funds has been charged. What you can do instead is process a refund request so that the funds will be returned to the customer. Please contact Veritrans support in order to submit a refund request.

What does a NOSCORE transaction mean?

A NOSCORE transaction means this transaction has been rejected by the bank for various reasons eg. insufficient funds, expired card, etc.
